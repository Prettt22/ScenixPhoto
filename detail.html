The Most Beautiful Mountains in the World
GoTravelly
October 27, 2023
3 Comments

The most beautiful mountain in the world has its own charm and spectacular views. Mountains are one of the tourist attractions visited by many tourists. You can get peace and fresh air when on holiday in the mountains. Besides being able to enjoy the charming views, you can also relax more.
By going to the mountains you can release all the fatigue that is on your mind. This is very necessary so that you do not easily experience stress or depression. Taking a vacation can also make you refreshed and increase your productivity when you get home. It's not surprising that many people choose to vacation in the mountains because it is very relaxing.
There are many of the most beautiful mountains in the world that you can visit. Every mountain is in the highlands, but some mountains have different locations and have their own uniqueness. You will feel a different sensation every time you visit the mountain. Here are some mountains with spectacular views in the world.

1. Mount Fuji, Japan 2. Mount Arenal, Costa Rica 3. Mount St. Helens, USA 4. Mount Vesuvius, Italy 5. Mount Krakatoa, Indonesia 6. Mount Etna, Italy 7. Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania 8. Mount Popocatepetl, Mexico 9. Mount Thrihnukagigur, Iceland 10. Mount Bromo, Indonesia

  • 1. Mount Fuji, Japan

    This mountain is very famous because it is a symbol of Japanese culture. This is what makes tourists curious and want to visit there. The peak of this mountain is covered with quite thick snow. Apart from that, there are 5 lakes in the surrounding area. This mountain is the center of Japanese history and culture. Around this mountain you can see old temples and also remains of ancient cultures from ancient times. This is a different attraction and is the centerpiece of the beautiful scenery there. The best time to visit this mountain is around June to August.

  • 2. Mount Arenal, Costa Rica

    This mountain area is surrounded by Mount Arenal National Park which is the best place to enjoy the beauty of lava flows. You also enjoy various natural beauties such as various species of flora and fauna. This mountain is very comfortable as a vacation spot, there you can do various interesting activities. One of the exciting activities you can do is riding a bicycle, swimming, white water rafting and so on.

  • 3. Mount St. Helens, USA

    This mountain is famous for its eruption which claimed many victims. During the eruption in 1980, 57 people died and damaged the surrounding area for 600 km. This active volcano is quite dangerous because it has very strong eruptions. However, this mountain is charming because it is part of the Volcanic Mountain National Monument. This is what makes thousands of tourists come to this mountain every year. However, visitors are only allowed to travel up to a distance of 6.4 km from the crater location.

  • 4. Mount Vesuvius, Italy

    is located in a bay called the Bay of Naples. This active volcano last experienced an eruption in 1944. This mountain is part of the Vesuvius National Park which has an important role in local culture and mythology. There you can see the ruins of previous eruptions.

  • 5. Mount Krakatau, Indonesia

    This mountain is one type of active mountain in Indonesia. has a charming attraction because it is part of the Ujung Kulon National Park. So it's not surprising that many tourists want to visit there. In 1883 this mountain experienced a quite violent eruption. As a result of the eruption, volcanic fluid spread and destroyed several areas around it. This liquid finally hardens and seems to form a new mountain.

  • 6. Mount Etna, Italy

    is still active today and often emits lava or ash. This mountain is located in the center of Sicily. has a peak covered with snow and inside there are 40 craters. This view is truly amazing and you can't find it anywhere else. This is what makes many people visit this mountain. It has a height of 3,329 meters above sea level, making this mountain the highest mountain in Europe. This mountain also rents out jeeps and cable cars to make it easier for tourists to get to the top.

  • 7. Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

    This mountain has a height of 5,895 meters above sea level. From this height, you can enjoy spectacular natural beauty. This mountain looks unique because it has three peaks with different panoramas. This mountain has a safe path to climb so it will not be difficult for visitors. This makes your holiday even more enjoyable. As many as 20,000 people visit there every year. On this mountain you can see biodiversity and also many types of animals that live there.

  • 8. Mount Popocatepetl, Mexico

    This mountain is also known as Mount El Popo in Mexico. This is an active mountain located 70 km southeast of the city center. In 2013 there was an eruption on this mountain. Even though it is an active mountain, this mountain has beauty at its peak. At the top of the mountain there is a National Park which attracts many tourists to visit it. There is a monastery that has been built since the 16th century. This monastery was built by Spanish monks who lived on the slopes of the mountain.

  • 9. Mount Thrihnukagigur, Iceland

    gets the title as the most amazing mountain because it has very charming views. This volcano has been inactive since 4,000 years ago. So it is estimated that this mountain will not be active again. This is what makes this mountain the most amazing tourist attraction. The uniqueness of this mountain is that tourists can explore the inside of the mountain. You can go 120 m into the inner area to reach the center of the mountain.

  • 10. Mount Bromo, Indonesia

    The most beautiful mountain in the world has often become the main destination for tourists. Apart from having cool air, this mountain has a beautiful panorama. You can enjoy the best sunrise from this mountain. There are even beautiful hills in the surrounding area that you can use as photo spots.

  • Not just on holiday, many people also come there to take pre-wedding photos. The charming view makes many people want to immortalize important moments there. After getting some recommendations for the most beautiful mountains in the world, you can start making a list of which mountains you want to visit first. To be able to go to the mountains you need to prepare yourself and complete supplies. Some mountains have quite steep and uphill roads, for this you need to use proper footwear.
  • You will feel a different sensation when on holiday to the most beautiful mountain in the world. You can even scream as much as you like or meditate on the mountain to get peace in your mind. That way, you can come back refreshed and live life more enthusiastically. Apart from that, you can also find out more about the many interesting panoramas throughout the world.
